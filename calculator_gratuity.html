Decoding the Terms

Last Drawn Salary

Includes only Basic Salary + Dearness Allowance (DA). It strictly excludes HRA, Bonuses, Incentives, or Overtime pay.

Years of Service

Total time spent with the employer. Under law, if you work more than 6 months in your final year, it is rounded UP to the next year.

The 15/26 Factor

The law considers 26 working days in a month. Gratuity is calculated as 15 days of salary for every completed year of work.

Rules & Regulations

Eligibility
Requires minimum 5 years of continuous service.
Exemptions
The 5-year rule is waived in cases of Death or Disability.
Rounding Rule
10 years 7 months is treated as 11 years.
Tax Exemption
Gratuity received up to the statutory limit is tax-free.
