Meaning of Terms
Gross Income

The total sum of your earnings from salary, business, interest, and any other sources before any deductions are applied.

Standard Deduction

A flat ₹50,000 deduction allowed to all salaried employees. This reduces your taxable income automatically.

Rebate 87A

A special tax waiver. In the New Regime, if your taxable income is ₹7,00,000 or less, your tax becomes zero.

Numerical Example

Profile: Salary of ₹10,00,000 (New Regime)

1. Taxable = 10,00,000 - 50,000 (Deduction) = 9,50,000
2. 3L to 6L (5%) = 15,000
3. 6L to 9L (10%) = 30,000
4. 9L to 9.5L (15%) = 7,500
Total Slab Tax = 52,500
Add 4% Cess = 2,100
-------------------------
FINAL TAX = ₹ 54,600

✅ Result: The tax is calculated on every rupee within specific slabs to minimize the burden.
