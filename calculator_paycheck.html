Components Explained
EPF Contribution

Calculated as 12% of your (Basic Salary + DA). This is your mandatory contribution toward your retirement corpus.

TDS (Tax Deducted at Source)

The monthly portion of your annual income tax. We calculate your annual tax first, then divide by 12 for the monthly deduction.

Standard Deduction

A flat ₹50,000 is subtracted from your annual gross salary automatically before tax is calculated. This is available in the New Regime.
