Decoding the NPS Components

Monthly Contribution

The fixed amount you set aside every month. NPS utilizes power of compounding, so even small amounts like ₹5,000 can grow into crores.

NPS Corpus

The final "Wealth Pot" accumulated at age 60. This includes your total principal invested plus the market-linked returns earned over decades.

Annuity (The Pension Engine)

The portion of your money you "give back" to an insurance company at retirement in exchange for a guaranteed monthly salary for life.

Latest NPS Rules & Regulations

Age Criteria: Open to all Indian citizens aged 18 to 70 years.

Withdrawal Rule: At 60, you can take 60% cash (Tax-Free). 40% MUST be used for pension.

Tax Benefits: Additional tax deduction of ₹50,000 under Section 80CCD (1B).
