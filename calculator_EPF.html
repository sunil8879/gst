Terminology Breakdown

Pensionable Salary

The average of your basic pay + DA over the last 60 months. For most, this is capped at ₹15,000 by EPFO rules.

Pensionable Service

The total years you contributed to EPS. If you work for 20 years or more, EPFO adds 2 bonus years to your total.

Divisor 70

A standardized mathematical factor set by the government to convert your total service and salary into a lifetime monthly benefit.

EPFO Rules & Regulations

10-Year Rule
You must complete minimum 10 years of service to be eligible for a monthly pension.
58-Year Rule
Normal pension starts at 58. Early pension starts at 50 with a 4% reduction per year.
Rounding Rule
6 months or more service in the final year is rounded up to 1 full year.
Min Pension
The Government of India guarantees a minimum pension of ₹1,000 per month.

Calculation Logic

Example: The "Bonus" Scenario

An employee earns ₹40,000 salary but is capped at ₹15,000 for EPS. He works for 20 years and 7 months.

1. Rounding: 20y 7m → 21 Years
2. Bonus: Service ≥ 20y, so 21 + 2 = 23 Years
3. Formula: (15,000 × 23) / 70
4. Result: 3,45,000 / 70 = ₹4,928 per month

✅ If he retires at 58, he gets the full amount. At 55, this is reduced by 12%.
